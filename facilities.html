World-Class Infrastructure

Every facility at Malabar College of Engineering is designed to nurture creativity, learning, and well-being.

📚

Central Library

Over 20,000 books across technical, management, and general literature. Subscriptions to leading journals and digital e-learning platforms.

🔬

Trade Laboratories

Fully-equipped ITI labs with latest industrial machinery, electrical panel training rigs, and civil drawing studios for practical learning.

💻

Computer Labs

High-speed internet connected PCs running latest operating systems, design software, accounting tools, and programming environments.

🏠

Hostel

Separate hostels for boys and girls with furnished rooms, 24/7 security, clean sanitation, common study halls, and regular meals.

🚌

Transportation

Fleet of college buses covering key areas of Calicut city and surrounding districts for daily commuting of students and staff.

⚽

Sports & Athletics

Multi-sport grounds including football, volleyball, kabaddi, and indoor facilities for table tennis, chess, and carrom competitions.

📡

Campus Wi-Fi

High-speed gigabit Wi-Fi across all academic blocks, library, and hostel. Enabling seamless online learning and digital resources access.

🎓

Smart Classrooms

Interactive digital boards, projectors, and audio-video teaching tools in every classroom for enhanced, engaging learning experiences.

🍽️

Cafeteria

Clean and hygienic canteen serving freshly prepared nutritious meals and snacks at subsidised rates throughout college hours.

🎭

Auditorium

Air-conditioned 500-seat auditorium equipped with professional audio-visual systems for convocations, cultural events, and seminars.

💊

Medical Room

On-campus health centre with trained nursing staff, basic medicines, and tie-up with nearby hospitals for student emergency care.

🌿

Green Campus

Eco-friendly campus with plantation drives, solar panels, rainwater harvesting systems, and waste management programmes.
