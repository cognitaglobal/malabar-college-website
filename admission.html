How to Apply

Follow these simple steps for a smooth admission experience.

1

Register

Create your admission account by registering with your name, mobile number, email address, and a secure password.

2

Fill Personal Details

Enter your personal information including date of birth, gender, address, contact details, and guardian information.

3

Academic Details

Provide your educational qualifications, examination details, board or university, marks, and year of passing.

4

Choose Your Course

Select your preferred programme or course from the available options and review the eligibility requirements.

5

Submit Documents

Upload the required documents such as passport-size photo, ID proof, academic certificates, and mark sheets.

6

Payment & Submit

Complete the application fee payment through the available online payment methods and submit your application successfully.
