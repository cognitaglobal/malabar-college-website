📋 Eligibility Criteria

ITI Trades

Minimum SSLC (Class 10) pass with Science and Mathematics. Age: 14–25 years. Relaxation for SC/ST/OBC applicants per government norms.

SIT Courses

Plus Two or equivalent pass for diploma programmes. SSLC pass for certificate courses. Basic computer literacy preferred.

SMS Programmes

Graduate degree in any discipline for PG programmes. Plus Two for undergraduate management certificates.

Cognita — Study Abroad

IELTS/TOEFL scores required depending on the destination country. Our team assists with language preparation.
