Life at Malabar College of Engineering Campus

A vibrant community where students learn, grow, compete, and celebrate together.

🎭

Cultural Events

Annual Arts Festival, inter-college competitions, and vibrant cultural performances showcasing student talent in music, theatre, and dance.

⚽

Sports & Athletics

Competitive sports teams in football, volleyball, cricket, kabaddi, and athletics participating in district and university championships.

🌿

Green Campus Drives

Eco-club drives, tree plantations, waste management initiatives, and solar-powered classrooms making MCE a model sustainable campus.

🍽️

Campus Cafeteria

Freshly prepared vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals at affordable prices. The cafeteria is a popular gathering spot for students.

📚

Student Clubs

Photography, debate, IT, and entrepreneurship clubs providing extra-curricular development and leadership opportunities for all students.

🤲

Community Service (NSS)

National Service Scheme activities including blood donation camps, literacy programmes, and neighbourhood community service drives.
