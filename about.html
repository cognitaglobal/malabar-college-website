Our Story Since 1922

A Legacy Built on Service and Scholarship

Malabar College of Engineering & Educational Institutions was established in 1922 by philanthropic Muslim leaders in Calicut, Kerala. Originally conceived as a shelter for orphaned children, Malabar College of Engineering rapidly evolved into a comprehensive educational organization encompassing schools, technical institutes, and colleges that have transformed over 70,000 lives.

Today, Malabar College of Engineering of Arts and Science stands as the flagship academic institution within the MCE family — offering ITI trade certification, IT diploma programmes, management studies, and global education pathways through Cognita, all under one esteemed roof.

Vision, Mission & Our Motto

Building future leaders through innovation, integrity and excellence.

Our Vision

To be a centre of excellence in skill-based and career-oriented education, empowering learners with knowledge, innovation, professional values, and global opportunities to build successful careers and contribute to society.

Our Mission

To provide quality, industry-oriented education through practical and technology-driven learning, equipping learners with professional skills, ethical values, and globally relevant competencies for lifelong success.

Our Motto

"Learn • Lead • Transform"

At Malabar College of Engineering, our motto reflects our commitment to empowering students through quality education, ethical leadership, innovation, and lifelong learning. We prepare confident professionals who contribute responsibly to society and shape a better future.
