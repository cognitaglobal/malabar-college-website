Alumni Association

The Alumni Association Committee serves as a cornerstone of connection and support within the Malabar College of Engineering community. It strengthens lifelong relationships between graduates and the institution while encouraging networking, mentorship, career development, and lifelong learning. Through alumni meets, technical workshops, mentorship programmes, and collaborative initiatives, the association continues to contribute towards the growth of both the institution and its graduates.
