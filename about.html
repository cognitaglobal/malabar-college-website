Our Story Since 1922

A Legacy Built on Service and Scholarship

Malabar College of Engineering & Educational Institutions was established in 1922 by philanthropic Muslim leaders in Calicut, Kerala. Originally conceived as a shelter for orphaned children, Malabar College of Engineering rapidly evolved into a comprehensive educational organization encompassing schools, technical institutes, and colleges that have transformed over 70,000 lives.

Today, Malabar College of Engineering of Arts and Science stands as the flagship academic institution within the MCE family — offering ITI trade certification, IT diploma programmes, management studies, and global education pathways through Cognita, all under one esteemed roof.

Our Journey Through Time

Key milestones that shaped Malabar College of Engineering into the institution it is today.

1922 — Foundation

Malabar College of Engineering established in Calicut by charitable Muslim leaders, providing shelter and basic education to underprivileged children.

1965 — ITI Established

Industrial Training Institute launched, affiliated to NCVT, offering certified trade courses in Electrician, Draughtsman Civil, and related streams.

1995 — IT Wing Launched

School of Information Technology (SIT) inaugurated, opening pathways to career-focused computer education for Kerala's youth.

2010 — Management School (SMS)

School of Management Studies introduced post-graduate management programmes, strengthening the field of business education.

2018 — Cognita Global Education

International education advisory wing, Cognita, opened connecting students to UK, Canada, USA, and Australian university pathways.

2022 — Centenary Celebration

Grand centenary celebrations marking 100 years of transformative educational and charitable services across Kerala.
