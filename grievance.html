About Grievance Redressal

Malabar College of Engineering maintains a formal Grievance Redressal Committee (GRC) as per UGC regulations to address and resolve academic, administrative, and personal complaints from students, staff, and parents in a prompt, impartial, and confidential manner.

All grievances are reviewed within 15 working days. The committee ensures no complainant faces any form of discrimination or retaliation for raising a legitimate concern.

Grievance Committee Members

  • 🎓 Principal / Chairperson
  • 📋 Student Welfare Officer
  • 👩‍💼 Senior Faculty Representative
  • 🎓 Student Representative (elected)

Types of Grievances Handled

  • 📌 Academic issues (marks, attendance, evaluation)
  • 📌 Fee-related disputes or refund requests
  • 📌 Misconduct or ragging complaints
  • 📌 Infrastructure / facility complaints
  • 📌 Anti-discrimination and equality issues

Submit a Grievance

All submissions are strictly confidential. You will be contacted within 15 working days.
